Credits
=======

STORM is derived from the `GLUE framework <https://github.com/gao-lab/GLUE>`__
(Cao & Gao, *Nat. Biotechnol.* 2022), with gene-program masking adapted from
`NicheCompass <https://github.com/Lotfollahi-lab/nichecompass>`__ and an
additional temporal-alignment objective.

The STORM icon was adapted from the original GLUE icon
(`glue vector by brgfx, www.freepik.com <https://www.freepik.com/vectors/glue>`__).
